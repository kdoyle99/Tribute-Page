Matthew Perry

August 19, 1969 - October 28, 2023

The timeline of Matthew Perry's life:

  • 1969 - Born in Williamstown, Massachussets
  • 1979 - First acting role in 240-Robert
  • 1987 - Graduated from college-prepatory school
  • 1988 - Starred as "Chazz Russel" in Boys Will Be Boys and in first movie, A Night in the Life of Jimmy Reardon
  • 1994 - Landed the role of "Chandler Bing" on the hit sitcom, Friends
  • 2006 - Won an Emmy for his performance in The Ron Clark Story
  • 2011 - Received a Champion of Recovery award for opening the Perry House
  • 2022 - Released his memoir, Friends, Lovers, and the Big Terrible Thing
  • 2023 - Died at the age of 54 at his home in Pacific Palasades, Los Angeles

"When I die, I don't want Friends to be the first thing that's mentioned — I want that to be the first thing that's mentioned. And I'm going to live the rest of my life proving that."

—Matthew Perry
